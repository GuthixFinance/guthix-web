How It Works

Three steps.
No management required.

sgxUSD is not a stablecoin. It is a token that appreciates passively as fees and yield accrue across the protocol's liquidity pools.

Step 01
🔄
Swap In
Swap USDC or USDT for sgxUSD via Jupiter or the GUTHIX interface.
Step 02
⏳
Hold
As fees and yield accrue across the pools, the value of each sgxUSD increases. No claiming. No staking. No active management.
Step 03
💱
Swap Out
Exit anytime by swapping sgxUSD back to USDC or USDT on Jupiter. No redemption queue.

Yield Sources

Five uncorrelated sources.
One token.

When one yield source compresses, the others carry the basket. Structural diversification — not cosmetic.

📈
Funding Rates
Perpetual Funding Rates
Delta-neutral positions across major venues capture perpetual funding rates. Highest yield in volatile or bullish markets.
🏦
Credit Yield
Institutional Credit Yield
On-chain institutional lending pools earn yield from real borrower demand. More stable than funding rates across market cycles.
🏛️
RWA Yield
Real-World Asset Yield
Short-duration US Treasury exposure. The most stable source in the basket — provides a yield floor independent of crypto market conditions.
🌉
Bridge Arb
Bridge Arbitrage Fees
Cross-chain stablecoin variants trade at persistent discounts to native USDC. Protocol pools absorb this arb flow continuously — structurally independent of yield markets.
💱
Swap Fees
Swap Fees
Every swap across all pools generates a fee that accrues directly to sgxUSD value. Scales with trading volume — independent of rates, credit, or bridge flow.

Risk Disclosure

Understand the risks
before participating.

⚠️
Experimental Software — High Risk
GUTHIX Protocol is experimental software provided "as-is." sgxUSD is a vault token whose NAV floats and is not guaranteed to appreciate. It is not a stablecoin, not a security, and not a guaranteed yield product. Participation may result in partial or total loss of funds. Smart contract code has been community-reviewed but has not undergone a professional audit at launch. A hard TVL cap is enforced until a professional audit is complete. Do not participate with funds you cannot afford to lose.
⚠ Smart Contract Risk
Despite community review, the protocol may contain bugs or vulnerabilities. Loss of funds is possible. A professional audit is planned but not yet complete at launch.
⚠ Market Risk
sgxUSD NAV floats and may decrease. Yield sources are volatile and not guaranteed. All five yield sources can compress simultaneously in adverse market conditions.
⚠ Counterparty Risk
Yield depends on Ethena, Maple, and Ondo. Failure or depeg of any collateral asset directly impacts sgxUSD NAV. GUTHIX does not control these protocols.
⚠ Liquidity Risk
Exits occur via secondary markets only. No direct redemption exists. Slippage may occur during high volatility. Protocol-owned liquidity depth may be insufficient for large exits.
⚠ Regulatory Risk
Laws governing digital assets are evolving rapidly. Regulatory changes may affect your ability to access or use the protocol. You are responsible for compliance with local laws.
⚠ Cross-Chain Risk
Bridging via Wormhole introduces additional smart contract and finality risks. Wormhole-bridged stablecoin variants carry independent depeg risk beyond the underlying asset.
